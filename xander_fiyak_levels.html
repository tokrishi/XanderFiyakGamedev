Main Levels Games

Levels

Borrasca

Supports a king of the hill style gamemode. Players fight over a crackhouse.

Taboo Coop

A cooperative map where players fight from objective to objective.
Includes multiple areas, including warehouses, a conviencene store, multiple other buildings, and a bridge.

Miasma

A single life map for small playercounts. Takes place in a ditch with a storage container on top.

Petrichor

Supports single life gamemodes. Includes an office building with a log cabin on one side and multiple buildings on the other.
Helped by Whisper Locs on Steam with enviromental design.

Hesitation

Supports a king of the hill style gamemode. Players fight over a house in Florida with a breezeway underneath.

Nail

Supports both single life objective gamemodes and a king of the hill style gamemode.
Takes place in an office complex surrounded by other buildings with a parking lot.
Helped by Whisper Locs on Steam with enviromental design.

Cult

A single life, objective based map. Attacking team has to enter the compound's courtyard and destroy the objective in the big building.
Helped by YouveSeenTheButcher on Steam with environmental design.
